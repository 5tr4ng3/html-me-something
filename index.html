Chapter One: The Lonely Duckling

When ducky was still an egg he rolled out of the nest into the cold rain. When the time came his brothers and sisters all hatched but not Ducky he needed more time. His momma waited a while before she eventually left because she thought Ducky didn't make it. But Ducky did make it and when he hatched his momma was gone and he was all alone. Because momma duck had left Ducky ended up imprinting on a frog.

Chapter Two: The Best Frogling Duck

Ducky wondered and wondered looking for his mommy but instead of momma duck he found daddy frog. Ducky imprinted on this frog and followed him everywhere. Ducky croaked like a frog, swam like a frog, ate like a frog. He even hopped like a frog. Ducky was the best frogling duck there was, probably because he was the only frogling duck around.

Chapter Three: Daddy Frog Passes

One day when Ducky was out and about eating and swimming having all the fun in the world daddy frog had an incident. Daddy frog was snatched up by a hawk, the hawk ate daddy frog and flew away. When Ducky returned home later he found out that daddy frog was no more. Ducky was very sad and didn't know what to do so he just started walking and never turned around.

Chapter Four: Ducky Finds Momma Duck

While Ducky was walking, and walking, and walking, and walking, and walking Ducky found Momma Duck. Ducky was so happy that he found his momma and ran around in circles out of happiness. Ducky and his family had a huge feast to celebrate. When they were done they went on there way to their new home just across the pond.

Chapter Five: Momma Duck Dies

Shortly after Ducky and his family were reunited they went home. A couple of days later momma duck was out in the garden gardening. When she noticed a shadow in the shape of a bird. She looked up and saw it was the hawk. The hawk swooped down and carried her away and ate her. Momma Duck was no more and Ducky was so sad that she was gone.
